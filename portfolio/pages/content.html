Summary

On this last theme assignment, I got the chance to do other things than coding. Don't miss understand me, I still like coding, but this time I had the opportunity to edit video which is very nice because I haven't done that before. So I learned a lot about how to edit video. I also learned how to film clips and what to think about next time. It is not easy to shoot all the clips at one time, and sometimes you think you have enough clips, but when you look at all the films you have, you find out you need more or better ones. As a team project, it was very nice to work from everyone, whenever everyone does not agree, or we discuss a lot. Also, I learned a lot from my group members. Every time I work on a group project, I learn so much about collaboration and teamwork. I develop a lot of personal because I have to be professional when we discuss or have to agree on something.
